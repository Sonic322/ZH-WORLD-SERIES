STREAMING GUIDLINES

  • Here are some basic guidelines for the World Series 2024. Every streamer has to set up a stream delay of at least 2 minutes up to a maximum of 5 minutes if the players request it before Stream start. You can do more but 2 minutes should be more than enough to avoid any kind of stream sniping accusations.

    Players are encouraged to check the stream delay with the streamer before the set.

  • It is not allowed to "reserve" several sets in advance. Feel free to message both players, ask them if you can stream and if it matches with their and your time post it into the WS topic and the following Stream Box. Every streamer can add his available timezones when "signing up" for GameReplays.org streams. You can of course stream as much games as you want in the earlier rounds. Replay casts are also very appreciated.

    For later rounds we as the WS Hosts will decide who can stream which set ( this will be based on connection, origin of the players, streaming efforts of streamers etc. ).

  • If you provide good coverage early on you will most likely end up getting bigger sets in the World Series later. Someone who streams one single set and then asks for a quarterfinal will most likely not have any kind of luck.

    While you are streaming you are the referee if noone else is around. This means be familiar with the WS rules ( AUTO REMATCH when Air vs any other USA) and the maps and map names. If you use a wrong map and players don't complain directly the set has to be played further. In case you have any issues with the players or any major issue regarding accusations, discussions etc. DM one of the WS Hosts and we will try to help.
